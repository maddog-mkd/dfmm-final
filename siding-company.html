homegrown company with deep roots!

DFMM Siding Company is a professional exterior finishing company serving Burlington, Oakville, Hamilton, Etobicoke, Mississauga and surrounding areas in the Greater Toronto Area. With over one million square feet of siding installed and thousands of linear feet of eavestroughing installed, DFMM Siding Company has the skills and experience to complete any job with professionalism.

We are committed to understanding our customers’ unique needs when it comes to their projects in order to achieve the highest level of success. We know the importance of protecting your home and we are proud to offer you a quality and cost-effective solutions NOW, and many years to come!

our philosophy!

Our goal is very simple! We want every job completed on time and on a budget! Once we start the job we tend to finish it as quickly as possible. We know the pride of homeownership. Our team of project coordinators are specifically trained to come up with the perfect plan that will fit our customers’ budget and design ideas.

safety as number one priority!

Safety is our number one priority. Our policy is followed by everyone who works for us and alongside us. We ensure that all measures are well taken to eliminate hazards and ensure the safety of our costumers, the public and our crew. Our employees are well trained and certified for their specific duties. Make sure your home is in good shape by having DFMM Siding inspect your siding. We strongly believe that giving your home an exterior makeover offers the upgraded look you desire.

Our experts are the ones you can trust for all of your siding needs in the Greater Toronto Area.
