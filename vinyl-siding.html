vinyl siding

Vinyl siding is a durable and beautiful option with a ton of great benefits! Do you want to enhance curb appeal and minimize maintenance requirements?

This is the perfect option for you!

what is vinyl siding?

Vinyl siding is a plastic exterior material for houses and small apartment buildings. It is used for decoration and weatherproofing, imitating wood clapboard, board and batten or shakes.

Many homeowners may find the siding options overwhelming However, one of the most common choices is Vinyl.

Why? Because it provides guaranteed and long-lasting protection.

This siding literally places a blanket of protection over the studs, keeping your home warmer in winter and cooler in summer!

what are the benefits of vinyl?

  • Affordability
  • Durable & Long-Lasting
  • Low Maintenance
  • Improves Curb Appeal
  • Quick And Easy Installation
  • Energy Efficient

Considering its beauty, durability, and affordability, it’s no wonder that more homeowners in Toronto and surrounding areas choose this material over any other option!

Low maintenance costs, low installation costs, and a lifetime warranty… overall, this is a very affordable exterior option!

Not to mention, vinyl and insulated vinyl siding are routinely among the top home improvement projects that give high returns on investment. In the most recent numbers from Remodeling’s Cost vs. Value analysis, a vinyl siding replacement offered an ROI of 80.7%. It made #4 on the list (out of 23 remodelling projects), topping both kitchen and basement remodels.
